Executive Synthesis

Quantum physics is the study of matter and energy at its most fundamental level. Unlike classical physics, where outcomes are predictable, the quantum realm is governed by probability and wave-like behavior.

Key Principles Identified:

  • Quantum Superposition

    The principle that a physical system exists in all its theoretically possible states simultaneously until it is measured.

  • Entanglement

    A phenomenon where particles become interconnected such that the state of one instantly influences the state of another, regardless of distance.

  • Wave-Particle Duality

    The concept that every elementary particle exhibits properties of both particles and waves.
